# God Skills Agentic Loop Graphics

**Goal:** Create nine blog-ready graphics for the God Skills operating model:
roster, workflow routing, and invocation guidance across clean diagram, mythic
poster, and ops dashboard treatments.

**Style:** HOPECODE x BC + AI fusion. Use solarpunk mycelial mapping, jittered
hand-drawn paths, analog paper grain, photocopy scars, earth tones, moss, rust,
ochre, spectral interference, and electric fungi glow. Keep BC + AI's rooted
professional clarity: deep forest green, coast blue, birch white, sunrise
orange, twilight purple, organic branching connections, clear hierarchy, and
at least 15 percent breathing room.

**Text policy:** Generated text is concept evidence only. Keep titles and short
labels large, sparse, and legible. Do not add unreadable body copy, fake URLs,
fake sponsor marks, fake dates, fake logos, or extra institutions.

**Respect policy:** Hindu names are mnemonic codenames only. Non-figurative
design only. Do not depict, imitate, parody, caricature, or impersonate deities
or religious scenes. Use abstract symbols and qualities, not portraits.

**Hard bans for every prompt:** deity portraits, religious parody, caricature,
literal divine impersonation, generic AI robots, glowing brains, corporate SaaS
dashboard wallpaper, sterile grid diagrams, hex-grid tech backgrounds, stock
icons, fake UI screens, fake metrics, fake sponsors, cluttered illegible text.
