# The Upgrade — Newsletter Heroes (upgrade style)

**Program:** The Upgrade (Newsletter)
**Style:** upgrade
**Reference:** none — relies on style suffix

Visual language: deep purple base, bright orange accents, light-blue tech highlights, white breathing room. Each hero expresses one editorial beat as a transformation metaphor — the imagery carries the theme so the same hero can ride above many different articles in that beat. Wide 16:9 framing with a clear focal point and at least one zone of calm where issue title or kicker can land.

Run: `python generate.py -f prompts/upgrade/newsletter-heroes.md -d output/upgrade-newsletter --style upgrade -m gpt-image-2 -w 2`

---

## 1. AI & Society — Weekly Cover

**For:** the catch-all weekly issue cover when the theme is broad cultural impact
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** upgrade
**Prompt:**
> Wide editorial composition: a diverse crowd of professionals in motion across an open plaza at golden hour, rendered with bold deep-purple silhouettes against a bright-orange and light-blue gradient sky. Above and between them, soft prismatic ribbons of light arc gently — suggesting an unseen current of change moving through the everyday. The people are walking, talking, looking up — alive and awake, not passive. Composition leaves the upper-left third open as breathing room for an issue title. The mood is awake and forward-leaning, never dystopian.

## 2. Tools & Workflows — Practical / Tactical

**For:** issues focused on hands-on AI tools, workflows, prompts, productivity techniques
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** upgrade
**Prompt:**
> A confident professional at a clean desk seen from a three-quarter angle, hands actively working between a laptop and a notebook, deep-purple and light-blue interface glow rising from the screen and flowing onto the desk surface like a gentle current. Bright-orange highlight accents trace the connection between hand, page, and screen — the human is clearly the conductor. Background dissolves into soft purple gradient with hints of upward arrows and subtle progress-bar motifs in light blue. Empty space along the right side for a headline. Approachable, capable, never cluttered.

## 3. Policy & Governance — Institutional / Legal

**For:** issues on AI regulation, policy, legal frameworks, institutional response
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** upgrade
**Prompt:**
> Wide composition of a modern civic interior — clean architectural columns rendered in deep purple, bright sunlight raking across a polished floor in warm orange. In the foreground, an open document on a desk catches the light, light-blue annotations and seal-like marks glowing softly across its surface. People are implied through long, calm shadows entering from the edges rather than depicted directly — the institutions, not the individuals. Negative space top-center for an issue title. The mood is serious but not heavy: civic life in motion, frameworks taking shape.

## 4. Human Stories — Personal Narrative

**For:** issues built around a personal account — someone whose work, life, or community changed because of AI
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** upgrade
**Prompt:**
> Intimate medium-wide portrait composition: a single person sitting in warm window light, seen from the side, expression thoughtful and present. Deep-purple interior tones surround them; bright-orange window glow falls across their hands and face. A faint light-blue thread of light runs from a small device on the table up and out the window — implying the technology is in their life, not the subject. The frame has clear breathing room above and to one side for a name kicker and headline. Honest, warm, dignified — never staged.

## 5. Builders & Makers — Creators Using AI

**For:** issues about artists, founders, engineers, and independents building with AI
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** upgrade
**Prompt:**
> Workshop scene rendered in bold editorial style: a creator's hands in the foreground assembling something — sketches, components, a half-built object — with bright-orange tools and deep-purple work surface. Above and around the hands, light-blue holographic guides and reference layers float at low opacity, suggesting AI-as-assistant rather than AI-as-author. Sparks and small upward arrows in orange punctuate the composition. The maker is clearly in command. Wide 16:9 framing with the right third open for a headline. Energetic, practical, full of agency.

## 6. Risk & Resilience — Failure Modes, Safety

**For:** issues on AI failures, safety practices, audits, harm reduction, recovery
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** upgrade
**Prompt:**
> Editorial composition: a single bright-orange compass or measuring instrument resting on a deep-purple surface, with light-blue diagnostic lines fanning outward in a careful grid — the tools of clear-eyed assessment. In the background, a soft purple gradient with a faint white horizon line suggesting the morning after, not the storm. No wreckage, no doom imagery — the focus is the discipline of measuring and adjusting. Negative space upper-right for a headline. The mood is grounded, vigilant, and quietly hopeful: this is the work of staying upright.

## 7. The Long View — Futures / Philosophical

**For:** issues that step back to ask bigger questions about meaning, work, agency, the long arc
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** upgrade
**Prompt:**
> Wide contemplative landscape: a single human silhouette in deep purple stands on a gentle ridge looking outward across an expansive horizon that gradients from deep purple at the base through bright orange to light blue at the sky. Distant, abstract architectural shapes — neither past nor future, just structure — sit on the far horizon in faint outlines. A few prismatic light particles drift across the middle distance. The framing is generous and open, with the figure offset to one side, leaving the horizon clear for a headline. The mood is patient, expansive, and reflective — wonder without nostalgia.

## 8. Communities & Movements — Collective Action

**For:** issues on community organizing, cooperatives, grassroots AI work, collective response
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** upgrade
**Prompt:**
> Birds-eye-into-three-quarter view of a circle of people gathered around a shared table, deep-purple silhouettes warmed by a bright-orange light source rising from the center of the table — the project, the shared work, the thing they are building together. Light-blue threads weave between them, connecting hand to hand and person to person, suggesting the network of mutual aid and shared knowledge. The composition is symmetrical and grounded, with breathing room at the top for a headline. The mood is warm, purposeful, and visibly collaborative — not a meeting, a movement.
