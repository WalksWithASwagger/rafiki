# ED + AI Logo Variations — AI Education Meetup

Brand extension for the **BC + AI Ecosystem**: a standalone identity for the
**ED + AI** education meetups that locks up cleanly with the parent BC + AI mark,
and is **evocative of Ethọ́s Lab** — the Black-led youth innovation academy that
hosts and partners on these meetups — without using their logo.

**Base Identity:**
- Parent brand: BC + AI Ecosystem (the `[X] + AI` family with the signature plus glyph)
- Sub-brand: **ED + AI** — the AI Education Meetup
- Naming logic: ED + AI sits in the same family as BC + AI, CV + AI, etc. The
  shared element is the **"+"** — same literal glyph, same accent color, same energy.
- Host / partner spirit: **Ethọ́s Lab** (Vancouver), evoked, not branded-in.
- Positioning: welcoming, practical, educator-led, community-rooted, future-ancestral.
- Audience: teachers, instructors, faculty, instructional designers, ed-curious builders.

**Design Goals:**
- A confident **ED + AI** wordmark that can stand on its own
- A descriptor line reading **"AI Education Meetup"** underneath
- A clear **lockup with the BC + AI logo** so the parentage reads instantly
- Reuse the BC + AI plus glyph so the family is obvious; the **"+" stays literal** everywhere
- An **even 50/50 fusion**: BC + AI's mycelial-network structure carried in warm
  Afrofuturist luminosity that nods to Ethọ́s Lab
- Works at avatar size and on an event banner

**Output root:** `output/ed-ai-logo/`

## The Ethọ́s Lab evocation (read before generating)

Ethọ́s Lab is a Black-led STEAM / AI innovation academy for youth in Vancouver.
We are **not** putting their logo in these marks. We **are** letting their spirit
warm the BC + AI ecosystem look. Translate it through energy, not appropriation:

- **Ubuntu — "I am because we are"** ↔ BC + AI's **mycelial network**. This is the
  bridge. Community as a living, interconnected web is the shared idea — lean on it.
- **Afrofuturism** (their youth are "Griots" building an Afrofuturist world,
  "Atlanthos"): future-ancestral, luminous, hopeful, imaginative — not retro-tech.
- **Griot / storyteller warmth**: knowledge passed between people, generous and human.
- **"A place to belong"**: welcoming, dignified, youth-positive.

**Cultural sensitivity guardrails:** abstract homage only. No Ethọ́s Lab logo,
no literal or borrowed cultural symbols (no Adinkra, kente, masks, or sacred
motifs rendered as decoration), no caricature, no people's faces in the logo.
Honor the spirit through palette, light, warmth, and the network metaphor.

## Fused palette (use this — no reference image required)

An even marriage of the BC + AI ecosystem palette and Afrofuturist warmth:

- **Deep evergreen / forest** `#1E3A2B` — structural base (BC + AI)
- **Coast teal** `#175E7C` — connective network, secondary nodes (BC + AI)
- **Birch white** `#F7F4E9` — clean space, contrast (BC + AI)
- **Luminous warm gold / amber** `#D4A017` — the **"+" glyph** and the
  Afrofuturist glow along the network (shared accent — the hinge of the fusion)
- **Copper / sunrise** `#D35C37` — warm energy, highlights (Afrofuturist warmth)
- **Deep aubergine / twilight violet** `#523E7B` — depth, cosmic future-ancestral
  tone (Afrofuturist warmth)

The **"+"** is always the literal BC + AI plus glyph, rendered in luminous gold.

**Text rule (logo work):** Baked-in text **is wanted** here. The wordmark must
read **ED + AI**, the descriptor **AI Education Meetup**, and endorsement lockups
may read **a BC + AI community** or **part of the BC + AI ecosystem**. Keep all
type crisp, correctly spelled, and evenly spaced — no garbled generated lettering.

**Shared negative constraints:** no garbled or misspelled text, no Ethọ́s Lab
logo, no fake/extra logos beyond BC + AI and ED + AI, no borrowed cultural
symbols as decoration, no faces in the mark, no generic AI clip art, no robot
heads or hands, no glowing brain circuits, no neon circuit-board cliché, no SaaS
gradient blobs, no stock conference imagery, no crypto/web3 vibes, no chalkboard
slogans, no children as the subject.

## Design Notes

**Family logic:**
- ED + AI is one of the `[X] + AI` BC + AI sub-brands. The constant is the "+".
- The "+" stays the **literal BC + AI plus glyph**, in luminous warm gold, everywhere.

**Fusion (even 50/50):**
- BC + AI gives structure: mycelial network, evergreen/teal, organic-professional.
- Ethọ́s Lab gives warmth: Afrofuturist gold/copper/aubergine luminosity, Ubuntu
  interconnection, Griot storyteller energy, "a place to belong."
- The hinge is the gold plus and the glowing network — present in both worlds.

**ED + AI identity:**
- Welcoming, practical, educator-led; community warmth over edtech sales gloss.
- Honor Ethọ́s Lab through spirit and palette only — never their logo, never
  borrowed cultural symbols, never faces in the mark.

**Typography:**
- Clean modern sans-serif, bold for "ED + AI", medium/light for the descriptor.
- "AI Education Meetup" sits directly under the wordmark, smaller, well-spaced.
- All caps reserved for seals and endorsement micro-copy.
