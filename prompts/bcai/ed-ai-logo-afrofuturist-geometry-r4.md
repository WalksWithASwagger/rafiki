# ED + AI Round 4 - Afrofuturist Geometry Logo Sprint

**Goal:** Explore cleaner, more original logo concepts for `ED + AI` / `AI Education Meetup` with an abstract Black futurist and Ethos Lab-adjacent sensibility: reciprocal community, youth worldbuilding, culture-based STEAM, pattern, rhythm, and luminous learning infrastructure.

**Creative correction from Round 3:** Less HOPECODE grit. No photocopy texture, no soil, no hand-scrawled field-poster energy, no heavy distress. This round should feel polished, luminous, future-facing, warm, and logo-system ready.

**Ethos treatment:** Abstract homage only. Draw from community, Ubuntu-as-reciprocity, youth agency, STEAM, speculative storytelling, and repeating geometry. Do not copy Ethos Lab marks, write Ethos Lab on the artwork, or borrow identifiable cultural symbols.

**Parent brand posture:** Use the attached BC + AI logo only as a small parent-brand / presenter reference and a plus-geometry cue. `ED + AI` is the visual engine. `BC + AI` may appear only as a tiny credit if it helps the concept.

**Text policy:** Rough generated text is acceptable for concept exploration. Prioritize the mark, wordmark architecture, and pattern logic over perfect lettering. Use only `ED + AI`, optional `AI Education Meetup`, and optional tiny `BC + AI`.

**Global art direction:** Clean vector-friendly logo concepts, refined edges, strong silhouette, extractable mark, minimal background noise, generous breathing room, original repeating geometric pattern language, warm human futurism, no generic startup AI aesthetic.

**Hard bans for every prompt:** robots, robot teachers, glowing brains, dashboards, holographic UI, generic node networks, hex-grid tech wallpaper, graduation caps, apples, school buses, lightbulbs, fake institutional seals, copied Ethos marks, the visible words `Ethos`, `Ethos Lab`, `Atlanthos`, `Ubuntu`, or `Griot`, kente patterns, adinkra symbols, masks, faces, sacred geometry, pan-African flag cliches, grit, grime, torn paper, photocopy distress, roots, soil, mushrooms, and full poster scenes.
