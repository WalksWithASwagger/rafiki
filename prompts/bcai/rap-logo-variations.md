# RAP Logo Variations — Responsible AI Professional Certification

Brand extension for BC + AI Ecosystem Association's first certification program.

**Base Identity:**
- Parent brand: BC + AI Ecosystem (navy blue, mint green, bright yellow plus sign)
- Program: RAP (Responsible AI Professional)
- Positioning: Professional certification, ethics-focused, globally applicable
- Audience: Executives, leaders, practitioners

**Design Goals:**
- Maintain BC + AI visual DNA (colors, organic aesthetic)
- Create distinct RAP sub-brand identity
- Professional yet approachable
- Works across digital and print

---

## 1. RAP Lockup — Stacked Certification Badge

**For:** Certificates, LinkedIn badges, email signatures
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (square)

**Prompt:**
> Professional certification badge design. Top half: "BC + AI" in mint green (hex #C5DFD5) letters with bright yellow (hex #E8ED4F) plus sign on deep navy blue (hex #0A1F3C) background. Bottom half separated by thin horizontal line: "RAP" in large bold letters, "Responsible AI Professional" in smaller text below, "CERTIFICATION" in smallest caps below that. All bottom text in mint green on same navy background. Clean, professional lockup with subtle organic curves in the dividing line (mycelial aesthetic). Minimal white space breathing room. Square format suitable for digital badge. No gradients, flat modern design with hint of natural flow.

## Design Notes

**Color Consistency:**
- Navy blue: #0A1F3C (from BC + AI logo)
- Mint green: #C5DFD5 (BC + AI text color)
- Bright yellow: #E8ED4F (plus sign accent)

**Typography:**
- Clean, modern sans-serif (Montserrat or similar)
- Bold weights for "RAP" and main text
- Medium weights for descriptive text
- All caps for "CERTIFICATION"

**BC + AI Connection:**
- Maintain color palette
- Reference the yellow plus sign as accent
- Subtle mycelial/organic elements (but professional, not wild)
- Clear visual relationship to parent brand

**RAP Identity:**
- Professional certification aesthetic
- Authority and credibility
- Ethics and responsibility visual cues (clean, transparent, trustworthy)
- Globally applicable (not overly regional)
