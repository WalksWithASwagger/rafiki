# ED + AI Round 5 - Independent Art Director Rebrief

**Art director diagnosis:** The previous round failed because "clean Afrofuturist geometry" plus the BC + AI logo reference turned into corporate innovation branding: tidy vector rings, polite sponsor marks, nonprofit conference energy. That is the wrong center of gravity.

**New center:** `ED + AI` is not a tech logo. It is a living learning signal: underground school, youth-made future alphabet, community signal system, speculative classroom artifact. Beautiful, strange, warm, memorable. More studio wall, zine table, math ritual, neighborhood constellation, future syllabus. Less AI summit sponsor lockup.

**Influence model:** Use Afrofuturist influence as structure, not decoration: recursion, call-and-response, collective authorship, future archives, relational intelligence, pattern as memory, youth agency, culture-based STEAM, community belonging. Avoid literal cultural borrowing.

**Creative correction:** Previous rounds felt either gritty HOPECODE or corporate vector tech. This round should feel human, strange, beautiful, community-made, Afrofuturist-adjacent, and identity-system fertile. Do not chase perfect production typography. Generate identity seeds with pulse.

**Reference posture:** Do not use BC + AI as the visual engine. For this exploration, `BC + AI` should not appear unless the prompt explicitly allows a tiny presenter note. Parent lockup can be added manually later.

**Text policy:** Use `ED + AI` as the hero text. Optional tiny `AI Education Meetup`. Treat generated text as rough concept evidence, not production lettering.

**Global hard bans:** corporate tech logo, SaaS mark, venture studio branding, generic innovation summit identity, stock nonprofit branding, robots, robot teachers, brains, dashboards, node networks, generic hex grids, graduation caps, apples, school buses, lightbulbs, fake seals, Ethos name/logo, Atlanthos text, Ubuntu text, copied cultural symbols, kente, adinkra, masks, faces, generic neon cyberpunk, sterile vector rings, glossy sponsor lockups, centered corporate badge symmetry.
