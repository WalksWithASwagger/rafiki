# ED + AI logo brainstorm - round 2

**Goal:** Generate a sharper brainstorming wall for the ED + AI / AI Education Meetup identity. This is not final-logo production. The point is to test distinct directions quickly across models while visibly anchoring the work to the official BC + AI ecosystem parent mark.

**Parent brand reference:** pass the official BC + AI ecosystem logo as `--global-reference-images` with `--reference-role brand`.

**Global guardrails:**
- The attached image is the official BC + AI parent brand reference. Use it as the parent-brand anchor.
- ED + AI is a sub-brand in the BC + AI ecosystem. The plus sign is the family signal.
- Keep visible text simple and intentional: `ED + AI`, optional `AI Education Meetup`, optional parent endorsement `BC + AI ecosystem`.
- Do not invent extra brand names, dates, venue names, sponsors, fake seals, QR codes, badges, or long unreadable copy.
- No robots, brains, hexagon grids, holographic dashboards, graduation caps, lightbulbs, generic SaaS blobs, or circuit-board clip art.
- No borrowed cultural symbols, masks, sacred geometry, nation-specific motifs, or faces. Warmth and community should come from composition, color, light, and material.
- Prioritize bold concept separation over polish. Each option should look like a different creative territory.

**For:** Primary sub-brand lockup concept

**Aspect Ratio:** square

**Style:** bcai-ecosystem

**Prompt:**
> Logo concept board for `ED + AI`, a sub-brand of the attached official BC + AI ecosystem mark. The `+` in `ED + AI` should feel like it grew directly from the parent mark's bright plus symbol: same square geometry, same luminous yellow-green energy. Add one simple spark or seed shape emerging from the plus, suggesting learning starting inside the ecosystem. Clean, bold, vector-friendly, not generic tech.
