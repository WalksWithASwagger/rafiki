## 10. Human in the Loop — Meaningful Oversight

**For:** Week 1 concept — keeping humans genuinely in control as the defining safety challenge
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** bcai
**Prompt:**
> Wide BC forest floor scene at dusk. A bioluminescent root network flows automatically in all directions — fast, purposeful, autonomous, covering the dark soil in organized light. But at one deliberate point in the network, the roots pass through a gap in the soil where a human hand rests gently — warm amber light emanating from the touch point, fingers slightly open. The hand is not stopping the network. It is present, watching, with the clear ability to close the gap. The contrast between the automated bioluminescent roots and the warm human touch is the whole subject. The hand looks genuinely engaged, not ceremonial.
