# ED + AI Round 7 - Education Remix From Starred Taste

**Goal:** Take one more serious crack at the `ED + AI` / `AI Education Meetup` identity by remixing the strongest prior ingredients into a more coherent education-first sub-brand under BC + AI.

**Taste board:** Use `output/ed-ai-logo-r7-education-remix/references/reference-board.png` as a taste board. It contains the official BC + AI parent mark, earlier pedagogy / HOPECODE-like field-note concepts, and newer aurora / bioluminescent / pop Afrofuturist concepts. Borrow the *logic* of the liked work: educational diagrams, field-note texture, handmade pedagogy, consent/commons/repair, aurora glow, bioluminescent signal, and BC + AI lockup posture.

**Do not copy the board literally.** The older generated `ED + AI` images are references, not assets. The only thing to treat as official is the BC + AI parent mark, and even that should stay small unless the prompt asks for a lockup study.

**Creative synthesis:** Hope Code field-notes, but less grimy. Aurora/bioluminescent pop, but less corporate. Afrofuturist sensibility through future learning, youth agency, rhythm, luminous pattern, and relational intelligence, not through borrowed symbols.

**Education thesis:** This is not generic AI branding. It is about education as practice: syllabus, timetable, classroom threshold, consent, repair, library, commons, curriculum, community care, and teachers learning together.

**Text policy:** `ED + AI` must be the hero. Optional `AI Education Meetup`. Small `BC + AI` parent mark or lockup only where useful. Generated text is rough concept evidence; final type will be manually rebuilt.

**Hard bans:** corporate tech logo, SaaS startup mark, generic innovation summit identity, sponsor-deck blandness, robots, robot teachers, brains, dashboards, generic node networks, hex-grid tech wallpaper, graduation caps, apples, school buses, lightbulbs, fake seals, Ethos name/logo, Atlanthos text, Ubuntu text, copied cultural symbols, kente, adinkra, masks, faces, generic pan-African flag cliche, glossy crypto/web3 vibes.
