**Program:** Responsible AI Professional Certification

**Style:** bcai

- Two signature lines at the base: "Kris Krüg, Co-founder, BC+AI Ecosystem" and "Program Director, Responsible AI Professional"

- Palette: deep forest green, warm gold, cream parchment / off-white
- Typography legible and crisp — this is a real document students hang on a wall
