## 1. Hero Image

**For:** Article header / LinkedIn featured image
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> An ocean of papers, text fragments, and cascading data files funneling through a narrow glass distillation column, emerging at the bottom as a single luminous crystalline document. Hands visible guiding the flow — not machines, a human curator. The contrast between the chaotic input and the pristine output is the entire message. Deep dark background (#0f0f1a), teal (#00c8b4) for the distilled crystal at the bottom, purple (#9333ea) cascading through the data above. Dark editorial illustration, high contrast, cinematic composition.

## 3. Section Image — The Distilled One-Pager

**For:** Solution section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**

Around it in deep shadow: the thousands of source documents that were compressed to produce it — their mass implied but reduced to darkness. The value of selection made visible. ## 4. Section Image — Gardening vs Hoarding

**For:** Framework section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> Split composition: left half shows an overgrown tangle of dead digital vines and wilted folders, file names barely legible — neglected storage, nothing tended or pruned.
