## 3. Section Image — Garbage In, Garbage Out

**For:** Technical principle section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> A beautifully engineered glass funnel — elegant, transparent, flawlessly constructed — mounted at center frame. Into its wide mouth: crumpled notebook pages, half-formed sticky notes with single vague words, a printout with an entire paragraph crossed out. Out of the narrow spout at the bottom: the same material, degraded further, compressed into pulp. The funnel itself is pristine and perfect. The problem is entirely the input. Dark background, the funnel lit from within by cold teal light, the degraded inputs and outputs rendered in grey-purple. Studio still life, slightly ironic, high contrast.
