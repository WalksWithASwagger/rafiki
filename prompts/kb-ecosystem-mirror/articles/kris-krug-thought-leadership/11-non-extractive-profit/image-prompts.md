## 1. Hero Image

**For:** Article header / LinkedIn featured image
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> A circular diagram, not hierarchical — value flowing in a complete ring, returning to its origin. Roots feed trees, trees feed soil, soil feeds roots. The circularity itself is the architecture. Deep dark background (#0f0f1a), teal and purple flow lines animating the cycle (#00c8b4, #9333ea), warm amber for the community nodes where value concentrates and redistributes. Dark editorial illustration, cinematic.

## 2. Section Image — Extractive Pipeline

**For:** Problem framing section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> A downward funnel — resources and value flowing in one direction only, never returning to source. Community at the top being steadily drained to profit at the bottom. The direction of flow is the problem made physical. Cool, austere, no warmth. The one-way arrow as the central visual fact. Dark editorial illustration.

---

## 3. Section Image — Regenerative Loop

**For:** Solution section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> Contrast to the extractive pipeline: a loop rather than a funnel. Value cycles — from community through product back to community. Each rotation amplifies rather than depletes. Teal arrows curving in a complete circuit, warm nodes at each transfer point, the feeling of return and reciprocity. Dark editorial illustration.

## 5. Closing / Social Image

**For:** End-of-article and social card
