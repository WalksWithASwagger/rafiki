## 1. Hero Image

**For:** Article header / LinkedIn featured image
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> A human hand hovering over a vast grid of AI-generated images stretching to the horizon, index finger poised to select one from hundreds of competent options. The selected image glows teal. The unselected ones fade to grey-purple. The gesture is small; the power is absolute. Curation as the rare thing. Deep dark background (#0f0f1a), teal on the selected (#00c8b4), grey-purple fade on the rest (#9333ea). Dark editorial illustration, cinematic.

## 2. Section Image — Generation Is Cheap

**For:** Problem framing section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> Hundreds of outputs spilling across the frame like an overturned deck of cards — all technically competent, none distinguished, none selected. The abundance problem made spatial. Cool, flat, overwhelming. No single image claims attention. The democratized mediocrity of unlimited generation. Dark editorial illustration.

## 3. Section Image — Selection Is Not

**For:** Reframe section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> A single image elevated from the grid of options, held up to a focused beam of light. The act of selection made ceremonial and rare. The held image glows teal, everything else recedes into shadow. Selection as the scarcity in a world of generation abundance. Dark editorial illustration.

## 5. Closing / Social Image

**For:** End-of-article and social card
