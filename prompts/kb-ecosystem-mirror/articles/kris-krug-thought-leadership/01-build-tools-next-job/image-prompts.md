## 1. Hero Image

**For:** Article header / LinkedIn featured image
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> A lone figure at a workbench in a dimly lit studio, sketching blueprints by lamplight. Through a floor-to-ceiling window behind them, an unfamiliar city skyline glimmers — buildings in shapes that don't quite exist yet, architecture slightly ahead of its time. Tools on the bench: some recognizable, some abstract prototypes. The figure leans forward with focus, not anxiety. Deep dark background (#0f0f1a), teal glow (#00c8b4) emanating from the future city, purple (#9333ea) pooling from the lamp. Dark editorial illustration, high contrast, cinematic depth of field.

## 2. Section Image — The Defensive Trap

**For:** Opening framing section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> A figure clutching a job title badge tightly to their chest, standing in a spotlight that's slowly shrinking around them. Shadow figures with updated credentials and glowing portfolios surround them at the edges of the dark frame. The metaphor of clinging to yesterday's value while the world redefines it. Teal and purple rim lighting from the surrounding figures, stark isolating white on the central figure. Dark editorial illustration, high contrast.

## 3. Section Image — The Offensive Move

**For:** Framework section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> The same figure now leaning confidently forward into a bright doorway, holding a fan of cards representing future skills — not playing defensively but dealing from a position of preparation. Purple light from behind them, teal light ahead in the doorway. The posture shift from defensive to generative is everything. Dark editorial illustration, cinematic.

## 4. Section Image — Six-Week Transformation

**For:** Story/case study section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> Split composition: left half shows a cluttered desk in week one — overwhelmed, tangled cables, blank screens, uncertain energy. Right half shows the same desk six weeks later — organized around new tools, artifacts pinned up, the person upright and engaged. Teal and purple light marks the transformation from left to right, the glow intensifying on the right side. Dark editorial illustration.

## 5. Closing / Social Image

**For:** End-of-article and social card
