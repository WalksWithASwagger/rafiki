## 1. Hero Image

**For:** Article header / LinkedIn featured image
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> A figure mid-leap — fully committed, both feet off the ground, arms open — against a dark backdrop. No net visible below, no safety structure, but the posture is joyful not panicked. Motion blur captures the irreversibility of commitment. Teal light from ahead, purple behind. The leap as the only logical response to an already-moving world. Deep dark background (#0f0f1a), teal ahead (#00c8b4), purple behind (#9333ea). Dark editorial illustration, cinematic.
