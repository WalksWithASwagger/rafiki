## 2. Section Image — Stealth Build (Opaque)

**For:** Problem framing section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> A figure working alone inside a sealed concrete room — no windows, door bolted from inside, the work itself obscured in shadow even from the viewer. The isolation is self-imposed and total. No light enters and no signal escapes. The figure has made the walls themselves part of the project. ## 4. Section Image — Authenticity Filter

**For:** Framework section
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** kk
**Prompt:**
> A physical filter mechanism at center frame — rendered as a brass valve or fine mesh sieve — with two streams approaching from the left. First stream: smooth, polished, performed content — generic phrasing, safe takes, risk-free observations — meets the filter and is blocked, deflected away. Second stream: opinionated, specific, clearly voiced work — passes through and emerges teal on the right. The filter rewards the real thing and stops the performed thing. Teal for what passes, grey-purple for what is blocked, the filter itself lit from within. Dark editorial illustration, high contrast.
