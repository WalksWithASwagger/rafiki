# Image Prompts: AI Animation Accelerator

AI image-generation briefs for the feature article and social media. All prompts should feel like they belong with the original Luma cover art (Tiny Ghost Studios claymation aesthetic), not generic corporate AI stock.

## Brand reference

The existing Luma hero image is the north star. Description of what's there so new art stays consistent:

- **Style:** Stop-motion / claymation. Sculpted, puffy, hand-crafted feel. Not polished CG, not flat vector, not photography.
- **Palette:** Deep purples and magentas, teals, electric greens, warm oranges and yellows from practical lamp light. Dark magical-studio backdrop.
- **Mood:** Playful, slightly spooky, handcrafted, creator-forward. Dark whimsy. Monster-friendly. Not sleek corporate, not sterile tech.
- **Characters:**
  - White friendly ghost (the Tiny Ghost mascot, bottom-right on logo). Cartoon, big eyes, simple smile.
  - Sculpted creature characters (dragons, goblins, little clay humanoids) as supporting cast. Think *Blood and Glitter* character aesthetic.
- **Typography:** "ANIMATION ACCELERATOR" is set in a bespoke 3D puffy/clay multi-color display face. Letters gradient through purple, pink, green, teal, yellow, orange. Letters have a hand-carved / claymation feel, not digital glow.
- **Setting:** An artist's attic studio. Shelves of clay busts, framed sketches and paintings on the walls, open sketchbook, pencils in jars, old books stacked, a warm desk lamp casting practical light.
- **Brand lockups:** BC + AI Ecosystem logo lower-left. Tiny Ghost Studios logo lower-right. Keep both present on any hero / cover variant.

When prompting: say "claymation," "stop-motion," "handcrafted," "textured clay," "dark whimsy," "purple magenta teal palette," "warm lamp light." Avoid: "clean corporate," "blue gradient," "neural network," "glowing hexagons," "AI chip," "holographic."

## Generation guidance

If using Midjourney / Flux / similar:

- Lead prompts with: "claymation, stop-motion, handcrafted clay characters, attic artist studio, purple and teal palette, warm lamp light, dark whimsy, hand-sculpted textures"
- Negative prompts / avoid: "corporate, vector, minimalist, blue tech gradient, neural network, hexagons, holographic UI, stock photo, people in suits"
- Reference image (image-to-image or style reference): the original Animation Accelerator cover at `https://images.lumacdn.com/cdn-cgi/image/format=auto,fit=cover,dpr=2,background=white,quality=75,width=400,height=400/event-covers/nt/0e6a1661-7a3e-4a36-ae9f-b3d5c1f866f5.png`

Logo placement: both BC + AI Ecosystem (bottom-left) and Tiny Ghost Studios (bottom-right) should be manually composited after generation, not prompted into the image, since AI image models will almost always garble them.
