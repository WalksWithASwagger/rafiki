# Image Prompts: AI Upgrade for Healthcare Professionals

AI image generation prompts for marketing the Healthcare Professionals program. All prompts follow The Upgrade brand aesthetic: dark mode, teal/purple accents, human-AI collaboration themes, modern and approachable.

---

## LinkedIn Post Graphics

### 1. Identity: The Clinical Companion
**Use case:** LinkedIn post about AI supporting clinical decision-making
**Dimensions:** 1200x628 (LinkedIn landscape)
**Prompt:**
> A healthcare professional (doctor/nurse) reviewing patient data on a tablet, with AI assistance subtly highlighting relevant patterns, suggesting considerations, summarizing records. The clinician remains the decision-maker. Warm clinical environment with teal AI highlights on screen. Style: modern healthcare, human-centered technology.

**Variations:**
- Doctor at bedside with AI-enhanced chart access
- Nurse station with intelligent patient monitoring
