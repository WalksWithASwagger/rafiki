# Image Prompts: AI Upgrade for Creative Professionals

AI image generation prompts for marketing the Creative Professionals program. All prompts follow The Upgrade brand aesthetic: dark mode, teal/purple accents, human-AI collaboration themes, modern and approachable.

---

## LinkedIn Post Graphics

### 1. Identity: The Wrong Question
**Use case:** LinkedIn post about "Will AI replace me?" vs "How do I become irreplaceable WITH AI?"
**Dimensions:** 1200x628 (LinkedIn landscape)
**Prompt:**
> A creative professional (photographer/designer) standing confidently at a crossroads, one path dark and foggy labeled with fear, the other illuminated with teal and purple light showing collaboration. Modern minimalist style, dark background with gradient lighting, cinematic composition. The figure holds both a traditional camera and a glowing AI interface hologram. Mood: empowering, forward-looking.

**Variations:**
- Same concept but with a graphic designer at a digital workstation with dual monitors showing AI tools
- Abstract version: two doors, one closing (fear), one opening with light streaming through (opportunity)
