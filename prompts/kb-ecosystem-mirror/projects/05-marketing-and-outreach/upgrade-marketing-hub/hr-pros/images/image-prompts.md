# Image Prompts: AI Upgrade for HR Professionals

AI image generation prompts for marketing the HR Professionals program. All prompts follow The Upgrade brand aesthetic: dark mode, teal/purple accents, human-AI collaboration themes, modern and approachable.

### 1. Identity: The People-First Paradox
**Use case:** LinkedIn post about AI giving HR time back for human connection
**Dimensions:** 1200x628 (LinkedIn landscape)
**Prompt:**
> An HR professional freed from administrative screens, now having a meaningful conversation with an employee. AI handles the paperwork visible in background, human handles the person in foreground. The paradox: more tech = more human time. Style: workplace relationship, technology-enabled humanity.

**Variations:**
