# Image Prompts: AI Upgrade for Journalists

AI image generation prompts for marketing the Journalists program. All prompts follow The Upgrade brand aesthetic: dark mode, teal/purple accents, human-AI collaboration themes, modern and approachable.

**Use case:** LinkedIn post about AI enabling more bylines, not fewer
**Dimensions:** 1200x628 (LinkedIn landscape)

The byline glows with the journalist's unique identity. Newspapers, websites, newsletters spreading outward. Style: media landscape visualization.
