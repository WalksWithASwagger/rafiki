## 1. Variation — Cosmic skull garden (symmetry shield)

**For:** Front chest print, white or dark garment mock area behind art

**Prompt:**
> Original front graphic for apparel. Text must read exactly: "VANCOUVER" arched across the top and large "AI" centered below, or a single arched line "VANCOUVER AI COMMUNITY" — pick one layout and make it legible. Style: authentic hand-drawn psychedelic 1960s/70s lot-tee rock poster art. Drippy, warped custom bubble lettering with thick black outlines and melting lower edges; gradient fills with subtle halftone/stipple grain like vintage screen print. Center: human skull with cranium open into a glowing cosmic nebula with stars. Red roses and green leaves frame the piece in a heart-shield silhouette. Left: fly agaric mushroom. Right: detailed CPU chip with warm glow. Yellow lightning bolts pointing inward from sides. Dark starfield behind the letters. High saturation, weird not corporate, strong contrast so it reads on chaotic tie-dye. Solid clean margin around the whole design as if for a shirt front.
