## Design Notes

**Color Consistency:**
- Navy blue: #0A1F3C
- Mint green: #C5DFD5
- Bright yellow: #E8ED4F
- Earth brown: #78552B (grounding)
- Coast blue: #175E7C (connections)
- Birch white: #F7F4E9 (breathing room)

**Typography:**
- Bold sans-serif for headings
- Italic serif only inside speech bubbles
- Caps for question numbers and section labels

**Aesthetic Rules:**
- Mycelial organic curves, never straight connector lines
- Circular nodes with soft radial gradients, subtle drop shadow
- Minimum 15% white space on every canvas
- No corporate grids, no robot/brain-circuit clichés, no clip art
