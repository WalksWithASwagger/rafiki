## 12. Fusion — gravure ring + field map + coast palette

**For:** Flagship blend; hardest prompt

**Prompt:**
> **Three-layer read** (disciplined, not collage chaos): (1) **Outer**: BC palette **radial network** very faint—forest green lines, orange nodes, lots of void. (2) **Middle ring**: **HOPECODE** jittered mycelial **field map** on warm paper tone, mostly **marginal**—like a torn inset. (3) **Inner core**: **biolum gravure**—macro lamellae arc occupying only **center 45%**, cyan/violet edge light, **true black** pit. Typography hierarchy locked: **ED + AI** largest on cream inset; **AI IN EDUCATION** across middle torn edge; **BC + AI** smallest. One **unifying** horizon line so layers relate. **Ban:** three unrelated styles stacked with no shared geometry; lens flare; hex grids.
