# Debbie Krug — "Pull Over" Album Artwork

**Reference style:** Warm travel-journal folk art. Think vintage road trip poster meets
Irish watercolor meets tall-tale Americana. Warm amber, working green, slate blue.
Sunlit, humorous, human. No dark tech aesthetics.

Run: `node index.js prompts/debbie-krug/album-artwork.md --output-dir output/debbie-album --ref prompts/debbie-krug/debbie-ref.jpg --style none -m gemini-2.5-flash-image`

## 1. Album Cover — The Passenger Seat

**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1

**Prompt:**
> Vintage folk-art album cover: a joyful woman with big sunglasses and wind-blown hair leans out
> the passenger window of a retro RV on an open American highway, arm raised in triumph, mouth
> open mid-shriek of delight. The highway stretches toward a horizon dotted with giant roadside
> sculptures — a huge buffalo, an enormous otter, a massive lobster. Warm amber sky, long
> afternoon shadows. Hand-lettered title "PULL OVER" arcs across the top in bold warm-gold
> letters. The style is vintage 1970s folk-art poster meets Americana travel illustration,
> warm ochre and sage green palette, slightly weathered texture.

## 2. Album Cover — The Navigator Has a List

**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1

**Prompt:**
