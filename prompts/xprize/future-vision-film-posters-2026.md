## 1. THE REPAIR SUN
**For:** Future Vision XPRIZE poster bakeoff
**Aspect Ratio:** 9:16
**Prompt:**
> Film poster, near-future Earth, sunrise over a reclaimed industrial coastline now transformed into a community reef-farm and solar-mangrove sanctuary. Avoid: dystopia, war imagery, corporate logos, sterile lab, bleak skies, weapons.
