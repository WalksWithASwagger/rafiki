## Principles

1. **Cosmic intelligence** — Constellations: connection over categorization. Orbit trails, radial nodes, space-blue gradients, glowing paths.  
2. **Editorial meets engineering** — UX overlays, dashboards, funnels, calendars, article modules, metrics woven into story.  
3. **Emotion in the machine** — People feeling, not only systems doing. Emojis, quotes, personas, comment clouds (sparingly).  
4. **Insight from chaos** — Dots → patterns → story. Prisms, filters, swarm-to-signal.  
5. **Playful precision** — Science-fiction journalism, not flat corporate deck; concept characters allowed (space miners, AI librarians).
