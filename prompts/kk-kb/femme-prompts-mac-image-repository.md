## Femme / body-compute set (polished prompts)

### Uterine Operating System (Soft Infrastructure)

- A dark computational field where contour lines behave like muscle tissue: gentle contractions ripple through a womb-shaped negative space without ever drawing the womb.
- Subtle vascular heatmaps bloom and fade like hormonal tides.
- Palette: deep indigo/black with blush-magenta, warm amber, faint teal.
- Quiet, intimate, non-symbolic.
- **Avoid:** goddess iconography, hearts, obvious pregnancy silhouettes.

### Cycle Clock (28-Day Algorithm)

- A circular phase diagram that *isn’t* a moon calendar: ring buffers, thresholds, and feedback loops expressed as gradients and micro-textures.
- The “cycle” is felt through asymmetry, not labels.
- Palette: night blues → rust reds → pearly whites → spring greens.
- **Avoid:** moons, period-blood clichés, calendars, text.

### Egg Logic (Potential Without Personhood)

- A luminous ovoid field like an ovum as a probability container.
- Membrane as a thin computational boundary with tiny error-corrections along its edge.
- Inside: soft particulate swirl, like readiness compiling.
- Palette: obsidian + opalescent pastels (peach, lilac, seafoam) with sparse gold sparks.
- **Avoid:** babies, embryos, literal cell diagrams.

### Placental Interface (Exchange Layer)

- Two interpenetrating networks, one warm and one cool, braiding without merging.
- Boundary behaves like selective permeability: slow gradients, semi-transparent veils, capillary-like filaments that read as negotiation and care.
- Palette: burgundy/umber vs cyan/ultramarine with iridescent boundary haze.
- **Avoid:** medical-illustration look, umbilical-cord literalism.

### Milk-Light Data (Nourishment as Signal)

- A soft fluid simulation where luminous “white” behaves like information.
- Striations like fiber optics inside a liquid bloom.
- Gentle, protective, directional without being a stream.
- Palette: ink black + pearlescent whites with subtle rose + pale blue interference.
- **Avoid:** breasts, bottles, maternal clichés.

### Body as Sanctuary (Protective Geometry)

- A mesh field folding around an implied torso shape.
- No figure drawn: only the way space tightens and relaxes, like fabric learning the body.
- Protective constraints appear as smooth attractors, not cages.
- Palette: charcoal with warm skin-spectrum gradients (peach, copper, muted coral) and faint violet.
- **Avoid:** armor, cyberpunk tropes, hard edges.

### Touch Memory (Fingertip Archive)

- A precise grid that has learned softness.
- Repeated micro-pressures accumulate as ridge-valley biases.
- Include faint shimmer where “contact” used to be.
- Palette: matte black/graphite with arterial reds and quiet blue highlights.
- **Avoid:** literal fingerprint patterns, biometric UI clichés.

### Mitochondrial Matriline (Ancestry as Glow)

- Cellular “powerhouses” as floating lantern-forms.
- Nested membranes like concentric contour maps.
- Vibe: lineage and continuity, not sci-fi organs.
- Palette: deep space + ember orange, hot pink microflares, jade glints.
- **Avoid:** textbook mitochondria, DNA helix front-and-center.

### Emotional Compute (Care-Based Intelligence)

- A network that routes not for efficiency but for gentleness.
- Thicker pathways stabilize fragile nodes.
- Detours form around stress zones.
- Intelligence reads as relational, not optimized.
- Palette: midnight base with saturated pops (fuchsia, tangerine, turquoise, lemon).
- **Avoid:** brain silhouettes, circuit-board motifs.

### Becoming Vessel (Identity as Fluid Architecture)

- A figureless portrait: a vertical field where layered translucent membranes drift, align, misalign.
- Identity assembling through cycles.
- Subtle halo-like interference patterns, no mystic symbols.
- Palette: black + oil-slick iridescence (green/purple/blue) with warm undertones.
- **Avoid:** chakras, sacred geometry, crystals, faces.
