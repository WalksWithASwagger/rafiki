## Short body (~90 words)

**Data as Protest. Story as Payload.**

Vancouver AI’s Data Storytelling Hackathon uses a custom dataset from Rival Technologies. Your job: turn numbers into something people can feel—visual, narrative, tool, or performance. First round’s prompt is the ridiculous “Is a hot dog a sandwich?”; the work is serious: what the data hides, who it includes, and how you make that legible.

**$10,000** total across four rounds (**$2,500** per winning submission). **Round 1** submission deadline **April 16, 2025**; awards **April 30, 2025** at the Space Centre. At least one team member must have been to a Vancouver AI meetup before the award date.
