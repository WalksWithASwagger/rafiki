# Wikipedia “Five Futures” project — image prompt archive

**Purpose:** Preserve complete image-generation prompts used in a Wikipedia futures / scenarios project (“Five Futures”), organized by theme and scenario.  
**Formats present:** Midjourney-style `/imagine` lines (some with `—c 10 —ar 2:3`), plus freeform **master prompt** + **subtheme** blocks for five narrative scenarios.  
**Ingested:** 2026-04-27  

**Note (archival):** Prompts are stored **as authored** (including satirical, dystopian, or person-referencing imagery). They are fiction / scenario art direction, not factual claims. Use care when regenerating or publishing; some **Resource Squeeze** and **Contributor Drought** entries use dark or sensational metaphors by design.

## Scenario set B — five narrative futures (master + subthemes)

### 1. Dystopian future due to AI misinformation

**Master prompt:**

> Create an image depicting a dystopian future overwhelmed by AI-generated misinformation, inspired by cyberpunk aesthetics. Visualize a world where fragmented realities coexist, featuring people surrounded by misleading, personalized digital content in a chaotic, technologically advanced urban landscape.

**Subtheme prompts:**

1. An individual lost in a sea of conflicting digital news streams, expressing confusion and distress.
2. A cityscape with holographic misinformation ads, showcasing a blend of futuristic technology and disinformation.
3. A group of people arguing, each with a different version of AI-generated news displayed on personal devices.
4. An AI bot presenting false historical data, with visuals of distorted, erroneous historical events.
5. A family in their home, each member isolated in their own bubble of personalized digital content.
6. A market scene where all advertisements are misleading, created by rogue AI systems.
7. A classroom where students are taught with AI-generated educational materials that are subtly incorrect.
8. A person trying to navigate a virtual reality world filled with deceptive AI-created illusions.
9. A hacker trying to correct AI misinformation, surrounded by screens showing the spread of fake news.
10. A protest against AI misinformation, with demonstrators holding signs depicting their struggle for truth.

### 2. Decline in Wikipedia contributors

**Master prompt:**

> Visualize a scenario depicting the decline of Wikipedia contributors, showing an aging generation of editors juxtaposed with empty, neglected digital archives representing the lack of new volunteers.

**Subtheme prompts:**

1. A lonely, elderly Wikipedia editor at a large, deserted computer lab symbolizing the absence of new contributors.
2. A visualization of Wikipedia pages turning to dust, representing the loss of active contributors.
3. A group of young people ignoring Wikipedia, engaged in other digital activities, highlighting generational disinterest.
4. A visual metaphor of an old, overgrown library with Wikipedia's logo, signifying neglect and obsolescence.
5. A timeline showing the gradual aging and decrease of Wikipedia contributors.
6. A contrast image showing vibrant, crowded social media platforms versus a deserted Wikipedia editing page.
7. A retired Wikipedia editor reminiscing about the platform's heyday, with a backdrop of fading digital archives.
8. A symbolic image of a baton being dropped instead of passed, representing the failure to attract new Wikipedia contributors.
9. A visualization of an 'edit' button slowly eroding, symbolizing the decline in content updates.
10. A collage of empty editorial desks with Wikipedia's logo, signifying the lack of volunteer engagement.

### 3. Reader exodus and changing information consumption

**Master prompt:**

> Create an image that illustrates the shift in information consumption, showing Generation Z engaging with modern platforms like TikTok and Instagram, while Wikipedia becomes an artifact of the past.

**Subtheme prompts:**

1. A group of teens completely absorbed in TikTok and Instagram, with Wikipedia on an old, unused computer in the background.
2. A modern digital museum exhibit displaying Wikipedia as an obsolete relic.
3. A split-screen comparison of a vibrant, interactive social media platform and a static, unused Wikipedia page.
4. Generation Z students in a classroom using TikTok for research, ignoring traditional web sources like Wikipedia.
5. A visual metaphor of Wikipedia as an old, dusty book on a shelf, overlooked in favor of shiny, new digital devices.
6. A family where the parents are using Wikipedia on a PC, and the kids are on TikTok on their smartphones.
7. A collage showing the evolution of information consumption, from books to Wikipedia, and finally to social media apps.
8. A visual contrast between a lively, colorful social media feed and a monochrome, deserted Wikipedia page.
9. A futuristic library where holographic social media apps are prominently featured, and Wikipedia is a small, forgotten section.
10. A teenager teaching an older person how to use social media for information, with Wikipedia in the background, unused.

### 4. Financial sustainability and big tech exploitation

**Master prompt:**

> Depict a scenario where big tech companies exploit Wikipedia for profit, visualizing Wikipedia as a dried-up well and tech giants as thriving, monetizing its content.

**Subtheme prompts:**

1. A giant tech company's logo overshadowing a withering Wikipedia symbol, signifying financial exploitation.
2. A visualization of a tech giant siphoning content from a fragile, depleted Wikipedia reservoir.
3. An image of Wikipedia as a dry, cracked earth, with tech companies as clouds raining money elsewhere.
4. A tech executive overlooking a field of Wikipedia content being harvested for profit.
5. A scale unbalanced, with Wikipedia on one side and a pile of gold coins with tech logos on the other.
6. A visualization of AI algorithms extracting data from Wikipedia, represented as a tree being stripped of leaves.
7. A scene of Wikipedia volunteers laboring in a field, while tech giants collect the fruits of their work.
8. A dystopian factory where Wikipedia content is processed and packaged for big tech profit.
9. A digital Robin Hood figure attempting to redistribute wealth from tech giants back to Wikipedia.
10. A boardroom scene with tech executives planning how to utilize Wikipedia content for commercial gain.

### 5. Victory scenario — Wikipedia's long-term success

**Master prompt:**

> Create an image celebrating Wikipedia's multi-generational success, visualizing it as a flourishing tree of knowledge, with diverse people benefiting from its vast, accessible information.

**Subtheme prompts:**

1. A thriving garden with a tree bearing Wikipedia's logo, symbolizing growth and knowledge.
2. A multi-generational group of people joyfully gathering under a tree with leaves representing different Wikipedia articles.
3. A futuristic cityscape with Wikipedia integrated into every aspect of life, from education to entertainment.
4. A Nobel Prize ceremony with Wikipedia being awarded, surrounded by a diverse, appreciative crowd.
5. A scene of children and elderly learning together from an interactive, holographic Wikipedia display.
6. A global map showing interconnected nodes, symbolizing Wikipedia's worldwide impact and accessibility.
7. A time-lapse image showing Wikipedia's evolution from a simple website to a global knowledge hub.
8. A utopian classroom where students of all ages engage with Wikipedia in a high-tech learning environment.
9. A mural depicting key historical and cultural figures contributing to a Wikipedia-branded wall of knowledge.
10. A celebration scene with people from diverse backgrounds sharing and contributing to Wikipedia, symbolizing community and collaboration.
