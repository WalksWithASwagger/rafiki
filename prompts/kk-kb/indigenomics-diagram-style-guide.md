# Indigenomics Institute — diagram & infographic style (condensed)

**Intent:** Bridge **ancestral wisdom** with **emergent technology**; make complex ideas intuitive; foreground **sovereignty, reciprocity, emergence**. **Co-design** sensitive visuals with Indigenous partners—this file is a **prompting aid**, not permission to invent sacred iconography without review.

**Gallery (images on Google Photos):** https://photos.app.goo.gl/BENttuCuvbNakjYA9

---

## Tone

Mythic realism meets cyberpunk; cosmic yet grounded; **humans-in-the-loop** and **land-based intelligence**.

---

## Materials & motifs

- Braided sweetgrass, cedar, copper wire; fiber-optic threads, circuit traces, node networks  
- Organic: rivers, smoke, branches, feathers (use only with cultural care)  
- Architectural / cultural references: longhouse, drum, council fire, salmon, mountains, eagles — **only when appropriate and reviewed**

---

## Geometry

Radial symmetry, circular flows, blueprint grids + organic overlays, data streams / ripples, isometric scenes mixed with simple pictographs.

---

## Palette (suggestions)

| Role | Hints |
|------|--------|
| Sky / spirit | Deep navy, indigo, midnight blue |
| Earth / ancestry | Burnt sienna, cedar brown, ochre |
| Tech / flow | Electric cyan, teal glow, copper, soft gold |
| Data overlay | White smoke, light grid blue, warm neon accents |

Glow = activated tech, data pulse, or **relationship**—use sparingly, on purpose.

---

## Typography

Titles: all-caps serif or slab (gravitas). Body: clean sans. **Minimize** text baked into images; prefer overlays for section titles only.

Sample title lines (adapt, don’t copy blindly):

- A New Indigenous Economic Architecture  
- Operationalizing Indigenous Values Through AI  
- From Data Shadows to Spotlight  
- Economic Self-Determination in Action  
- Setting the Pace, Not Catching Up  

---

## Diagram archetypes

1. **Blueprint fusion** — Longhouse + community circle + smart city (infrastructure + values)  
2. **Living AI brain** — Organic brain + circuitry (value-aligned processing)  
3. **Geo-mapping layer** — Canada / territory map with glowing nodes (visibility)  
4. **Flow river path** — Values → design → capital → outcomes  
5. **Race metaphor subverted** — Lead with Indigenous youth / futures (avoid “catch-up” framing)  

---

## Data representation

Prefer **relational** charts (flows, cycles, reciprocity) over only transactional bar charts. When possible, **shape** charts with meaningful forms (e.g. salmon, cedar) with **community review**.

---

## Delivery

SVG + PNG; square or vertical; transparent variants; layered source when available.

**Iteration questions for the team**

- What teaching does this represent?  
- Where is the human or the land?  
- How does this reclaim narrative power?  
- Readable by both youth and Elder?

## Section prompts (library excerpts — paraphrase in production as needed)

**Petroglyphs to code** — Leader at center of glowing data circle; petroglyphs one side, code the other; constellations suggest $100B (verify numbers with Institute).

**Technology in service of sovereignty** — Interface echoing drum or bentwood box; hand activates traditional pattern ripples; text only if on-brand.

**Braided AI brain** — Sweetgrass, cedar, copper, circuitry; inputs from land, water, people; outputs as salmon/eagle/mountain-shaped charts (metaphor, not literal data fabrication).

**From data shadows to spotlight** — Map of Canada with nodes + organic overlays; “satellite” lens on Indigenous-led enterprise (wording to match program).

**Blueprint: longhouse + circle + skyline** — Fiber-optic threads weaving connection.

**River flow** — Community → Design → Capital → Outcomes; council fire, laptop, handshake, growing cedar.

**Pace, not catch-up** — Subvert race: birch-bark track, future bikes with beadwork/regalia nods; youth lead ahead of generic robots. **Review** for appropriation vs celebration.
