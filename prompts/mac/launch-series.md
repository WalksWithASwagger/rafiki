# MAC — Mind, AI & Consciousness — Launch Series

**Purpose:** First batch of brand-identity tiles for the MAC subgroup of BC + AI, generated in the
new `mac` style. Six invented episodes mapped one-to-one onto the six MAC visual motifs, in the
existing monthly cadence (Jun–Nov 2026).

**Two modes per the brief:**
- **Background-only** (model `flash`): reliable, reusable abstract art with clean negative space
  top-left + bottom-left for later text overlay in a template.
- **Text-baked** (model `gpt-image-2`): MAC wordmark + "Mind, AI & Consciousness" + colored kicker
  baked in. Looks finished but text fidelity varies — spot-check and regenerate as needed.

**Series:**
1. Emergence: When Does Information Become Experience? — Jun 2026 — particle field
2. The Embodied Turn: Why Minds Need Bodies — Jul 2026 — contour topography
3. Predictive Selves: The Brain as Forecasting Engine — Aug 2026 — line-field portrait
4. Attention Is All You Are — Sep 2026 — radial kaleidoscope
5. Substrate Independence: Can Mind Run on Silicon? — Oct 2026 — iridescent mesh
6. The Hard Problem, Revisited — Nov 2026 — spectrogram wave

**Run:** `python generate.py -f prompts/mac/launch-series.md -d output/mac-launch -s mac -w 3`
