## Overall Slide Palette

- Background: #000000 (black) or #F5F0E8 (off-white newsprint)
- Primary text: #FFFFFF (white on black) or #1A1A1A (near-black on off-white)
- Accent: #E63B2E (punk red, used sparingly — 2-3 slides max)
- No gradients. No drop shadows. No rounded corners on text boxes.
- Font stack: Franklin Gothic Heavy for headers, Courier New or similar typewriter face for body.
