# Data Center Protest Signs — BLOCKY COLORFUL FUTURE-PUNK (remaining 11)

**Purpose:** The other 11 concepts, in the blocky / colorful / future-punk direction:
risograph overprint + neo-brutalist rave flyer + cyberpunk street poster. Full-bleed poster
artwork, NOT a photo of a sign.

**Render notes:** style=none. No wall, no background, no drop shadow — color and blocky type fill
the entire frame edge to edge. Exact sign text in quotes; spell it exactly, no extra words.
