# Data Center Protest Signs — ROUND 3 (weird & witty, future-punk)

**Purpose:** 10 new oddball "both-hands-full" lines — weird, witty, deadpan. NOT doom, NOT
hope-sermon, NOT thought-leader manifesto. Drawn in AND side-eyeing it. Blocky bold geometric
block-letter typography, vivid clashing future-punk color, risograph overprint + neo-brutalist
rave-flyer + cyberpunk poster. Full-bleed artwork, NOT a photo.

**Render notes:** style=none. No wall, no background, no drop shadow — color and blocky type fill
the entire frame edge to edge. Exact sign text in quotes; spell it exactly, no extra words.
