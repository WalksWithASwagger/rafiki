# Data Center Protest Signs — FLAT PUNK ARTWORK (remaining 11)

**Purpose:** The other 11 concepts from the original 20, in the same flat punk-zine artwork
direction as image-prompts-flat-punk.md. Print-ready, full-bleed, NOT a photo of a sign.

**Render notes:** style=none. No wall, no background object, no drop shadow — lettering and paper
grain fill the entire frame. Exact sign text in quotes; spell it exactly, no extra words.
