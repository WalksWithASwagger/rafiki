# Data Center Protest Signs — BLOCKY COLORFUL FUTURE-PUNK

**Purpose:** The 9 favorite lines, rebuilt from the ground up in a new direction: blocky bold
geometric block-letter typography, vivid clashing future-punk color, full-bleed poster artwork.
Think risograph overprint + neo-brutalist rave flyer + cyberpunk street poster. Loud, designed,
still DIY, still refusing to pick a side. Print-ready, NOT a photo of a sign.

**Render notes:** style=none. No wall, no background object, no drop shadow — color and type fill
the entire frame edge to edge. Exact sign text in quotes; spell it exactly, no extra words.
