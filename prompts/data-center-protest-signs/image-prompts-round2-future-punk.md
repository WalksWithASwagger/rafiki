# Data Center Protest Signs — ROUND 2 (blocky colorful future-punk)

**Purpose:** 10 new "both-hands-full" concepts — built to be iconic and screenshot-viral.
Blocky bold geometric block-letter typography, vivid clashing future-punk color, risograph
overprint + neo-brutalist rave-flyer + cyberpunk poster. Full-bleed artwork, NOT a photo.

**Render notes:** style=none. No wall, no background, no drop shadow — color and blocky type fill
the entire frame edge to edge. Exact sign text in quotes; spell it exactly, no extra words.

---
