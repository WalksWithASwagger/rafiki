# Data Center Protest Signs — ROUND 5 (constructivist edge × future-punk)

**Purpose:** The 8 locked tender lines, re-treated HARD. Russian constructivist agitprop
composition (Rodchenko / El Lissitzky) crossed with future-punk glitch. Type-forward, diagonal,
geometric, propaganda edge. NO cartoon faces, NO cute mascots, NO googly eyes. The tension
between soft words and hard treatment is the point.

**Shared look:** Flat full-frame protest poster, NOT a photo, bleeds to all edges. Bold dynamic
diagonal constructivist layout; heavy condensed blocky sans-serif type set at aggressive angles;
geometric bars, wedges, circles; photomontage fragments of machinery / turbines / server hardware
as hard geometric abstraction. Aggressive limited palette — constructivist RED, BLACK, bone-WHITE
— spiked with ONE electric glitch accent. Risograph overprint misregistration, halftone dots, RGB
chromatic glitch offset, scanlines, ink texture. Hard-edged, agitprop, not cute.

**Render notes:** style=none. Exact sign text in quotes; spell it exactly, no extra words.
