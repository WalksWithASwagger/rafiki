# The New God Flow - Book Cover Concepts

**Book:** *THE NEW GOD FLOW* - *A walk through nineteen albums and a tradition
older than the form* - Michael Caswell
**Style:** `new-god-flow`
**Render notes:** Aspect 2:3. Use the exact cover copy in quotes.

---

## 1. Iridescent Anatomical Vessel
**Aspect Ratio:** 2:3
**Prompt:**
> Front-facing human anatomical figure on matte black. Body surface is high-gloss iridescent oil-spill shifting purples, blues, and greens. White leader lines point to body parts. Top text: "THE NEW GOD FLOW". Subtitle: "A walk through nineteen albums and a tradition older than the form." Bottom text: "MICHAEL CASWELL". Reverent scientific medical chart, premium book-cover restraint.

## 2. Minimalist Silver Slipcase
**Aspect Ratio:** 2:3
**Prompt:**
> Book cover with clear translucent plastic slipcase texture over matte metallic silver. Center: single anatomical heart in sharp color-shifting holographic foil. Title "THE NEW GOD FLOW" in classical small caps. No photos. High-end industrial packaging, restrained and collectible.

## 3. Classical Linework Shimmer
**Aspect Ratio:** 2:3
**Prompt:**
> Detailed anatomical figure with fine cross-hatching in dark ink on warm pearlescent cream paper. Selective holographic foil highlights a "Divine Signal" flowing through the throat and nervous system. Title "THE NEW GOD FLOW" at top in elegant serif. Scientific, liturgical, and premium.

## 4. Prismatic Back-View Spine
**Aspect Ratio:** 2:3
**Prompt:**

**Aspect Ratio:** 2:3
