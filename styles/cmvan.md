# Creative Mornings Vancouver / Punk Rock AI Visual Style Guide

## Overview

Creative Mornings Vancouver / Punk Rock AI is the maximalist zine-collage direction for the May 2026 keynote and related release assets. It keeps the raw black/white/red discipline of punk print culture, then adds hot pink/magenta, fire, photographic objects, and dripping paint typography for a more chaotic live-event register.

Use it when the image should feel like a physical poster, slide, cover, announcement, or release-day artifact assembled from distressed paper, halftone photography, and loud hand-built type.

## Core Language

- Maximalist punk collage, not polished corporate event design
- Four-color discipline: black, white, blood red/crimson, and hot pink/magenta
- Photographic objects embedded in the collage: hands, boots, tools, fire, faces, paper, tape, and print fragments
- Huge dripping-paint words, stencil typography, torn typewriter fragments, marker scrawls, and ransom-note cut letters
- Political urgency with warmth and live-room energy

## Palette

- Pitch black and aged off-white
- Blood red / crimson
- Hot pink / saturated magenta
- No other colors

## Composition

- Dense, layered, and intentionally unstable
- At least four or five overlapping paper, photo, paint, and text layers
- Massive type can dominate, but the main subject must still read
- Use torn edges, angled fragments, staple holes, tape, ink drip, and spray bleed
- Backgrounds can alternate between black and aged paper

## Texture

- Xerox artifact, halftone dots, risograph misregistration, silkscreen grain
- Worn paper, coffee stains, visible fibers, ink smudges, spray paint bleed
- Photographic elements should feel real, then distressed into the print system
- Not flat vector stencil work

## Use For

- Creative Mornings Vancouver keynote slides
- Punk Rock AI posters and social cards
- Release Day templates
- Loud event announcements and cover images
- Physical-looking print artifacts

## Avoid

- Clean SaaS gradients
- Generic tech conference visuals
- Polished symmetry
- Extra colors outside black, white, red, and hot pink
- Literal dashboard UI or glossy robot imagery

## Example Prompt Add-On

```text
Render this in the Creative Mornings Vancouver / Punk Rock AI style: maximalist punk zine collage in black, white, blood red, and hot pink only, with distressed halftone photography, dripping paint typography, torn paper, tape, staple holes, spray bleed, fire energy, and dense overlapping layers that feel hand-assembled for a live creative keynote.
```
