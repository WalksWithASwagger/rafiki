# Futureproof Mythic Visual Style Guide

## Overview

Futureproof Mythic is the surrealist festival-world direction: Pacific Northwest folk myth, handmade symbolic density, living technology, and civic futures treated like a ritual invitation.

Use it when the image should feel like a poster, dream scene, talisman, cabinet of wonders, portal, council, or creature encounter.

## Core Language

- Surrealist PNW folk-art, not generic fantasy
- Watchful eyes, ravens, salmon, whales, orcas, bears, eagles, cedar forests, rivers, mushrooms, portals, councils, and thresholds
- Technology as living pattern: circuit tattoos, DNA constellations, mycelial roots, glowing data trails in water, bark, sky, or bodies
- Figures as archetypes: dreamer, watcher, inventor, elder, keeper, council, pilgrim
- Festival energy as a mythic gathering rather than a normal conference

## Palette

- Deep forest shadow
- Ocean teal and aurora cyan
- Coral pink and ember gold
- Cedar brown, moss, muted charcoal
- Bioluminescent accents used as signal, not decoration

## Composition

- One strong symbolic focal point
- Dense supporting details that reward inspection
- Imperfect symmetry is welcome; sterile grids are not
- Poster-like, storybook-like, or artifact-like framing
- Natural thresholds: archways, eyes, river bends, forest clearings, ferry crossings, seed vaults

## Texture

- Painted folk-art surfaces
- Analog grain, paper tooth, soft glow, mist, worn edges
- Handmade imperfection and slightly uncanny scale
- Avoid clean vector-flat execution unless the prompt asks for a badge or sticker

## Use For

- Mythic posters and hero images
- Track worlds and festival story scenes
- Surreal social cards
- Album/poster-like announcements
- Archetypal portraits
- Creature and portal concepts

## Avoid

- Generic fantasy armor, medieval tropes, dragons, castles, or RPG aesthetics
- Sterile SaaS gradients and corporate event stock imagery
- Literal robots, dashboard UI, and generic glowing brain imagery
- Overly polished symmetry that removes the handmade quality

## Example Prompt Add-On

```text
Render this in the Futureproof Mythic style: surrealist Pacific Northwest folk-art, symbolic and handmade, with living technology woven through natural forms, aurora teal and ember-gold light, analog grain, one strong mythic focal point, dense story details, and a tone of wonder, invitation, and build-what-lasts civic imagination.
```
