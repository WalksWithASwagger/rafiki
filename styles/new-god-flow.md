# New God Flow Visual Style Guide

## Overview

`new-god-flow` is an industrial minimalist luxury style for the book cover
**THE NEW GOD FLOW** (*A walk through nineteen albums and a tradition older than
the form*, by Michael Caswell). It treats the cover as a high-end record sleeve,
art monograph, or collector object: one stark ground, one precious accent, one
reverent subject.

## Color Discipline

- One ground: deep soft-touch matte black or pure non-reflective matte white.
- One accent: iridescent, holographic, dichroic, or oil-spill foil applied to a
  single focal element.
- Avoid extra accent colors that compete with the foil.

## Visual Elements

- Reverent anatomical subject: medical-chart linework, anatomical figure,
  heart, jaw, spine, nervous system, or historical engraving.
- Classical serif typography in small caps with generous spacing.
- Optional clear-plastic slipcase texture.
- Extreme negative space and centered composition.

## Tone

Sacred, scientific, premium, otherworldly, quiet, and authoritative.

## When To Use

- *The New God Flow* book cover and companion imagery.
- Luxury editorial covers built around restraint and one luminous focal point.
- Anatomical or "body as instrument" metaphors.

## When Not To Use

- Loud collage, punk, glitch, or maximalist layouts.
- Corporate deck visuals or generic technology branding.
- Images that need photographs of real people.

## Example

```bash
python generate.py -p 'Front-facing anatomical figure on matte black; body surface high-gloss iridescent oil-spill. Top text: "THE NEW GOD FLOW". Bottom text: "MICHAEL CASWELL".' --style new-god-flow -m pro -a 2:3
```
