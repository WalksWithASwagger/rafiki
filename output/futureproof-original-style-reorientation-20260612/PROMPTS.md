# Futureproof Original Style Reorientation

Generated on 2026-06-12 after the newer suites drifted too far into clean scenic Pacific Northwest fantasy.

## Reset Principle

The original Futureproof Festival style is the canon:

- dense salmon-current mass
- watchful eyes embedded in the current
- black ravens carrying violet crystal shards
- aurora pressure across a deep night sky
- ornate hand-painted folk-poster linework
- jewel-like highlights
- mystical but grounded Pacific Northwest energy
- poster composition first, landscape second

The next variants should be new and original, but they must feel like new panels from the same poster world.

## Avoid

- scenic realism
- generic fantasy landscape
- soft travel-poster mood
- corporate AI imagery
- robots, laptops, interface graphics
- flat vector art
- dirty/gritty texture
- distressed paper
- muddy brush noise
- pseudo-text, fake lettering, dates, logos, signs

## Shared Constellation Rule

Use accurate simplified northern-sky constellation line art where sky is visible:

- Cassiopeia: W shape
- Cygnus: Northern Cross
- Lyra: small parallelogram with bright Vega
- Pegasus: Great Square
- Andromeda: chain extending from Pegasus
- Cepheus: house/pentagon shape
- Ursa Minor: Little Dipper
- Delphinus: small diamond with tail

Preserve correct relative star topology. Do not invent constellation shapes.

## Source Concepts

### 01 Canon Salmon River Hero

Wide hero reset. Dense living river of salmon and dark teal current-strands rising through the lower half, watchful eyes, ravens, crystals, aurora, and real constellations. Text-free with upper sky space.

### 02 Canon Square Poster Base

Square social/poster base. Graphic density and strange festival energy from the original: patterned salmon, teal current ribbons, embedded eyes, ravens, crystals, aurora, and a calm lettering pocket.

### 03 Forest Doorway Salmon Current

Night forest threshold, but held in original language: salmon-current roots, eyes, ravens, violet crystal shards, aurora through canopy, glowing doorway. Not generic portal fantasy.

### 04 Salmon Current Footer Strip

Ultra-wide/footer support graphic. Partial salmon forms, dark teal current ribbons, watchful eyes, raven silhouettes, crystal fragments, narrow aurora star band. Calmer for overlay use.

### 05 On-Water Salmon Crossing

Water viewpoint variant. A small crossing silhouette is secondary; the real subject is the mythic salmon-current tapestry below, with eyes, ravens, crystals, aurora, and constellations.

### 06 Daylight Forest Salmon Canon

Daytime experiment, but still original-poster-first: a bright forest clearing where the ground is a living braided salmon-current path, with patterned salmon, watchful eyes, ravens, crystals, and ornamental inkwork.

## Production Notes

- All assets are text-free.
- Designer-controlled typography remains preferred.
- This packet is a calibration reset, not the final full campaign suite.
- If a variant feels pretty but not strange, it should be treated as off-canon.
